— Who I Am

About Me

I'm an aspiring engineer focused on building real-world skills in robotics, development, and creative tech.

My journey started with hands-on robotics using Arduino, sensors, and motors, where I built a sensor-based motor car (individual project) and worked on a robotic arm (group project).

Alongside hardware, I moved into web development, creating projects like Slice Frenzy, FunHub, and a Turf Tournament website.

I also explore video editing, design, and creative media — combining tech with creativity to build things that look as good as they work.

Outside tech, I was selected for a national-level football camp as a goalkeeper and left defender, building discipline and teamwork alongside my technical skills.
